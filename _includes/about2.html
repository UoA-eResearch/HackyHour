When and Where

Hacky Hour is an open and inclusive environment, all are welcome to join in. With support from the Center for eResearch there are knowledgeable folk around who can help you with your research or technical questions.

Join us from 3-4pm @ Strata Cafe every Thursday and continue the discussion on Gitter. Or follow us on Twitter. Bring along your research questions, latest tools to share, or things to hack on - along with your device if you have one - and we look forward to seeing you there!
